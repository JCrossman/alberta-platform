## üèóÔ∏è What You're Building

### Use Case 1: Healthcare Intelligence
- **Problem**: Emergency departments don't know when to expect high patient volumes
- **Solution**: AI predicts wait times 48 hours in advance using historical data
- **Value**: Better resource allocation, reduced patient wait times

### Use Case 2: Environmental Monitoring
- **Problem**: Manual monitoring of air/water quality is slow and reactive
- **Solution**: Real-time monitoring with AI-powered anomaly detection and forecasting
- **Value**: Faster incident response, public health protection

### Use Case 3: Citizen Service Chatbot
- **Problem**: Citizens can only get help during business hours, call centers overwhelmed
- **Solution**: 24/7 AI chatbot that answers questions about government services
- **Value**: Improved citizen satisfaction, reduced call center costs

### Use Case 4: Data Governance
- **Problem**: Don't know what data exists, where it came from, or who can access it
- **Solution**: Automated data cataloging, classification, lineage, and access control
- **Value**: Reduced compliance risk, faster data discovery, trusted data
